About Us

What Is Umeed ?

A small initiative taken to provide a better quality education to kids who belong to the marginalized sections of the society.

An attempt to secure the future of these kids, because they have both talent and interest towards education, but unfortunately do not have a good financial background.
We come as an “Umeed” (Hope) of a new morning in the lives of these kids and their families.

How We Work ?

We select kids, who are interested as well as intelligent in studies, but do not have a good financial background.

We get these children admitted into good institutions to ensure their good quality education. Not only this, We also provide them regular tuitions and take occasional tests to check their progress.
This scholarship will inspire other students to excel and take up a challenge to be one of the beneficiaries.
